This page tests navigation in IPFS deployments.

How to properly fix navigation in IPFS deployments:

  1. Include the ipfs-nav-fix.js script in the head of your HTML
  2. Make sure to include ipfs-fix.js after it
  3. Register the service worker with ipfs-sw.js
  4. Test navigation links to ensure they stay within your site and don't redirect to ipfs.tech
